Courses Page

OB Montessori Center has 3 courses. They are divided into Casa Program, Grade School Program, and Professional High School Program.

Casa Department Levels Age
Junior Casa Nursery 1, ages 3 to 4
Junior Advanced Casa Nursery 2, ages 4 to 5
Advanced Casa Kindergarten, ages 5 to 6
Grade School Department Levels Age
Primary Grades 1,2, and 3, ages 6 to 9
Intermediate Grades 4,5, and 6, ages 9 to 12
Professional High School Department Levels Age
Junior High School Grades 7,8,9, and 10, ages 12 to 16
Senior High School Grades 11 and 12, ages 17 to 18
